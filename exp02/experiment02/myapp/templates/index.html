camelCase vs Kebab-Case

Studies performed in natural language reading show that people read better (20% faster) when any explicit separator is used between words.

For example: The overall message is that «readingthistext» is more difficult than «reading_this_text» or «reading this text»

The goal of this experiment is to investigate whether code reading speed is affected by the type of identifier used (i.e. camelCase or kebab-case)

What should I do as a user?

Your task:

  1. Provide demographics information.
  2. Read a short sentence.
  3. Read a set of identifiers.
  4. Select the unique identifier corresponding to the sentence you just read.
  5. Complete 10 tasks, half in camelCase and half in kebab-case.
  6. Your answers and time-taken-to-answer are being recorded.
